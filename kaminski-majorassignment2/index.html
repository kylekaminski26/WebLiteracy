Background Information

Background Information

Nintendo amiibo are collectible NFC figurines that the consumer electronics company, Nintendo, have released.

These figurines have small NFC chips in their bases, which are very small storage devices that are capable of reading and writing data to and from a game for extra in-game functionality.

Functions like storing user data and unlocking bonus in-game content are the most common features from the wide array that amiibo provide to the collector.

Amiibo were first announced to the world on June 10th, 2014.

Although most casual consumers at first were hesitant to preorder, collectors went crazy, which in turn caused mass hysteria among consumers and collectors alike.

Why I Collect

Why I Collect

It all started in December 2017, when I went to NYC with my sister and her boyfriend.

Since I helped them pay for train tickets, her boyfriend offered to buy me something at the Nintendo Store, since we were heading there anyway.

Not knowing what to get, I just settled on a Pikachu Amiibo because I liked the way it looked.

I then realized that there were way more figurines of characters I liked.

A year and a half into collecting, I now stand at about 100 figurines that I have collected (some outside of the Smash Bros series that is listed above).
